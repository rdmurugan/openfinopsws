Historical Analytics & Data Persistence

Store and analyze historical cost data with flexible database backends. Query past metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions based on historical patterns.

  • Multiple Storage Backends
    Choose from In-Memory, SQLite, PostgreSQL, or TimescaleDB based on your needs
  • Historical Data Queries
    Query metrics and costs from days, weeks, or months ago for trend analysis
  • Configurable Retention
    Set data retention policies (90 days, 1 year, or custom) to manage storage
  • Time-Series Optimization
    TimescaleDB support with compression for efficient long-term storage

Plugin Architecture

    Register event hooks for pre/post processing and custom workflows
