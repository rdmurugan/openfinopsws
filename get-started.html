Frequently Asked Questions

Is OpenFinOps really free?
Yes! OpenFinOps is 100% free and open source under the Apache 2.0 license. No hidden costs, no premium tiers, no credit card required.
Which cloud providers are supported?
OpenFinOps supports AWS, Azure, and GCP. You can monitor costs across all three providers in a single dashboard.
How does the LLM-powered recommendation work?
OpenFinOps can integrate with OpenAI, Anthropic, Azure OpenAI, or Ollama to analyze your infrastructure and provide intelligent recommendations. You can also use rule-based recommendations without an LLM.
How long does setup take?
Most teams are up and running in under 5 minutes. Just install via pip, configure your cloud credentials, and launch the dashboard.
Can I use this for production workloads?
Absolutely! OpenFinOps is designed for production environments and is already used by teams running large-scale AI/ML workloads.
