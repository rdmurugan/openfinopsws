Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial for Enterprise editions?
Yes! All Enterprise tiers include a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required.
Can I switch between tiers?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
What happens if I exceed my tier limits?
We'll notify you when you approach limits. You can upgrade to the next tier or purchase add-ons. We never shut down your service without notice.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing?
Yes! Save 17% with annual billing ($500/month becomes $6,000/year for Startup, $2,000/month becomes $24,000/year for Business).
What payment methods do you accept?
Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), ACH transfer, wire transfer, and purchase orders (Enterprise only).
What's your refund policy?
30-day money-back guarantee for annual plans. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime (no refunds for partial months).
Can I use Community Edition commercially?
Yes! Community Edition is free for commercial use with no restrictions.
How fast is support response for Enterprise tiers?
Startup: 24 hours, Business: 4 hours, Enterprise: 1 hour (24/7).
Is there a limit on cloud spend I can track?
No! Track unlimited cloud spend on all tiers.
