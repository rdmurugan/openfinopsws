Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Community Edition really free?

Yes! The Community Edition is 100% free and open source under the Apache-2.0 license. You get full source code access and can use it for any purpose, including commercial use.

Can I upgrade from Community to Enterprise later?

Absolutely. You can start with the Community Edition and upgrade anytime as your needs grow. We'll help with the migration process.

What payment methods do you accept?

For Professional and Enterprise plans, we accept credit cards, bank transfers, and purchase orders. We offer annual and multi-year billing options.

Do you offer managed hosting?

Yes, we offer fully managed cloud hosting as part of our Professional and Enterprise plans. Contact sales for details.

What's included in support?

Community: GitHub issues and discussions. Professional: Priority email with 24-hour response time. Enterprise: 24/7 support via email and phone with a dedicated account manager.
